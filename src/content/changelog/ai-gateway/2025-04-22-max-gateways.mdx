---
title:  AI Gateway increases gateway limit to 20 per account
description:  AI Gateway paid users can now use up to 20 gateways per account 
date: 2025-04-22T12:00:00Z
---
Starting today, we have increased the maximum number of gateways for paid [AI Gateway](/ai-gateway/) users from 10 to 20. This gives you greater flexibility in managing your applications as you build and scale.

Don’t forget we still have [custom metadata](/ai-gateway/configuration/custom-metadata/) for even more detailed tracking. You can tag and filter requests with user IDs or other identifiers, enabling better tracking and analysis of your requests, so you don’t have to create one gateway per user.
